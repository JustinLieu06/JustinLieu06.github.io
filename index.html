A List of Fun Facts

  • 2012

    Hip Hop

    When I was a freshmen in Valencia High School, I developed a quick fascination with hip hop culture, particularly rap and break dancing. Since then, rap has become my go to genre of music where I love listening to all kinds of artists especially Logic, Kanye, Eminem, Kendrick, and many more. Furthermore, break dancing is still my favorite dance style to watch. Quest Crew and Morning of Owl are just some of my favorite dance groups.

  • 2013

    League of Legends

    My favorite game to play since High School has been League of Legends. My ranking in the game went from the bottom 3% to the top 0.29% in the North America region in the span of the first year I started playing. I love participating in the competitive atmosphere with my friends and recording clips of my gameplay to share with others.

  • 2016

    Fashion

    I've always had an appreciation for art, and find that fashion is a powerful tool that people can use to express themselves. I love looking at fashion subreddits and Youtube channels that go over modern fashion. I thoroughly enjoy following the fashion style known as streetwear which has become extremely popular among my age group.

  • 2018

    Boxing

    The first quarter I transferred over to SCU, I joined the boxing team where I instantly found my love for the sport. Boxing is not only enjoyable to watch, but also very fun to partake in due to the strategy, skill, focus, and dedication needed to best your opponent in the ring.
